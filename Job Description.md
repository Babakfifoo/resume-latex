# Job description

We are looking for Credit Control Analyst to join our Credit Control team in Global Business Services. In this role you will work in close co-operation with our European Sales Units to drive activities within your area of responsibility. This position is fixed-term until 30.9.2026 and based in our HQ in Espoo, Finland.

What you will do
Customer credit management activities
Cash collection
Analysing and reporting of open receivables
Participating in month end closing reporting in credit control area
Possibility to participate in finance project work and testing in credit control area
What we are looking for 
Finance/Accounting degree or equivalent experience
Experience and knowledge of Credit Control and Accounts Receivable processes
Strong communication skills. Excellent written and verbal English; Finnish and other languages are considered a benefit
Basic Excel skills
SAP experience is a plus
Proactive and flexible working style with self-driven work approach and can-do attitude
We value an excellent team player who likes working in an international and diverse environment
Ability to work in fast-changing environment with tight schedules
