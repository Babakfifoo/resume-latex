What you’ll be doing

As a Quantitative Risk Analyst, the specific role may vary based on qualifications, but you could i.e. play a valuable role in Data analytics, optimizing data flows, product management or as a data governance specialist. Your role could also include coordinating data related activities and ensuring proper communication within the teams and across other Risk Models areas.

What you could be doing:

Root-cause analysis for data quality issues, where we meticulously investigate and identify the underlying reasons for any discrepancies in our data. This is closely followed by data reconciliation, ensuring that our data sets are consistent and accurate across different sources.
We focus on optimizing data flows and algorithms to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of our data processes.
Developing analytical tools enabling better data analysis and decision-making.
Own and manage all data used in credit risk models across the end-to-end lifecycle, applying data product mindset to capture, enhance and realize value throughout credit risk models.
Identify and govern critical data elements though strong knowledge of credit risk models, regulatory expectation and capital.
Secure our Data Governance framework and processes with focus on effective Data Quality Controls, also ensuring documentation of Data Lineage toward all data providers and consumers for credit risk data
Preparing reports and other documentation for senior management and external reporting is also an integral part of our role, ensuring transparency and informed decision-making.

You’ll join an international team with variety of backgrounds and skills. We believe that strong drive and passion for change are fundamental for delivering on our commitments. Bring your background and skills to our team, and you will have plenty of opportunities to collaborate, grow and develop.

The role can be based in Denmark, Finland, Norway Sweden or Poland – and we expect that are you are already based close to any existing location with a main Nordea office
