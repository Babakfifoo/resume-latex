# About the role

Join our dynamic team and help us build, optimize, and maintain cutting-edge data integration solutions for global clients. In this full-time, client-facing role, you'll work closely with customers to understand their data needs, design effective solutions, and ensure smooth implementation for long-term success.

We’re looking for problem-solvers with strong technical skills and a passion for making data work in real-world applications. If you excel at translating complex requirements into practical outcomes and thrive in collaborative, client-facing environments, we’d love to hear from you.

We’re hiring multiple Data Engineers and welcome candidates with experience in data engineering, analysis, management, or similar fields especially those eager to transition into a customer-facing role.

Key Responsibilities

Gathering requirements from new and existing clients and designing integration pipelines
Implementing new data integration or updating and expanding existing ones
Provide excellent customer support and communication throughout the support lifecycle
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration and deployment of data solutions
Monitor, maintain, and optimize existing data integration solutions for clients
Requirements

Proficiency in SQL is a must-have skill
Basic experience with cloud technologies, preferably Azure services such as Azure Data Factory and Azure Databricks.
Strong customer support and communication skills in English
Excellent soft skills including logical thinking, troubleshooting, and investigation
Detail-oriented with the ability to carefully manage tasks and communication with stakeholders
Extra points if you have

Experience in Microsoft SSIS, SQL server and Python
Experience in a customer-facing role
Background in data engineering, data integration, data analysis, ERP consulting, or related fields
Procurement knowledge
What we offer

Competitive compensation package and a stock option program
Awesome perks include extensive health care, mobile phone connection, home internet plan, recreational benefit, referral bonus, bike benefit, and many more.
Opportunity to work in hybrid with a supportive team
True autonomy and responsibility. We are looking for people who yearn for this freedom, make the most out of it, and take on responsibility
Growth opportunities within the company, including training and development programs
