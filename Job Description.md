Role Overview: Data Engineers (Junior to Senior Levels)
We are seeking talented Data Engineers at both junior and senior levels. Your specific responsibilities will depend on your experience, technical skills, and growth trajectory. Join us to shape scalable, high-performance data solutions across a dynamic technology landscape.
Key responsibilities
Junior Data Engineers should have hands-on experience with physical data modeling and writing SQL for data warehouse or analytics use cases.
Senior Data Engineers are expected to bring deep expertise in dimensional data modeling and developing robust ETL/ELT pipelines using a variety of technologies.
If you are a technically advanced Data Engineer, you will focus on increasing automation, building reusable code components, and accelerating data product development-particularly leveraging Databricks and Python.
