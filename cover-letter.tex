I am now proficient in MySQL, Python, and various data visualisation tools, and I find myself particularly drawn to challenges that revolve around understanding human behaviour.

Whether through analysis, dashboards, or experimentation, it aligns perfectly with the hands-on work I aspire to pursue.

I am a quick learner, comfortable with ambiguity, and genuinely enthusiastic about Supercell's culture of small teams that embrace true ownership. I would greatly appreciate the opportunity to contribute.
